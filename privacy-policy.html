CatVerse Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 10, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how CatVerse ("the Application," "we," "us") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our application, **CatVerse**. We are committed to protecting your privacy and process your information only for the purposes described herein.

1. Types of Information We Collect

The Application does not actively collect any **Personally Identifiable Information (PII)**, such as your name, address, or phone number. However, to provide advertising and analytics services, we collect the following **Non-Personally Identifiable Information (Non-PII)** through third-party services:

Information Collected via HUAWEI Petal Ads (HiAdKit) SDK:

  • Device Identifiers: Advertising Identifier (OAID), device model, operating system version, screen density, network connection type (Wi-Fi/cellular).
  • Ad Interaction Data: Ad clicks, impressions, view duration, and performance evaluation.
  • Installed Apps List (QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES):

    To meet the anti-fraud requirements of the advertising platform and provide more accurate ad exclusion features, the HUAWEI Petal Ads SDK may access the list of installed applications on your device. **This data is strictly for the internal use of the ad SDK to: (a) identify and filter ads for already installed apps to enhance user experience; and (b) perform anti-fraud detection. The Application does not use this list for any other purpose or transmit it to our servers.**

Information Collected via Application Analytics Tools:

  • Usage Data: Crash reports, error logs, application open and close times, in-app interaction behavior, and operation flow.
  • Transaction Data (If In-App Purchases exist): Products purchased, transaction amounts, but **excluding** your payment information (which is handled by third-party payment providers).

2. Purposes for Using the Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing Ad Services: To display relevant ads within the Application via HiAdKit.
  • Optimization and Maintenance: To diagnose errors, analyze the Application's stability and usage trends to optimize the user experience.
  • Anti-Fraud and Security: To prevent ad fraud, detect malicious software, and enhance the overall security of the Application.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and resolve technical issues.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

Your Non-PII is only shared with the following partners, and the sharing purpose is strictly limited to providing the services described in this policy:

  • HUAWEI Petal Ads (HiAdKit) SDK: Used for ad delivery, measurement, and anti-fraud.
  • Third-Party Analytics Services: Used to analyze application performance and user behavior.

We do not sell your information to any third party unless explicitly required by law.

4. Your Rights and Opt-Out Choices

You can control your ad privacy settings at any time:

  • Opting Out of Personalized Ads: You can reset your Advertising Identifier or enable the "Opt-out of Ads Personalization" feature through your Android device's system settings to limit advertisers from using your data for interest-based targeting.
  • Contact Us for Data Deletion: If you need to inquire about or request the deletion of data related to your device that we hold, please contact us using the information below.
